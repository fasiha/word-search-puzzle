Word Search Puzzle

Step 1. Enter words below, separated by spaces or line breaks. Use Latin characters (ABC), ひらがな, or カタカナ.

Step 2. Enter the size of the grid (e.g., 5 for a five by five grid)

Step 3. Check/uncheck the directions the words can go in.

Step 5. Print. The puzzle and the key will take up the whole page.
